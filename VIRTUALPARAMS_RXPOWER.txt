# Virtual Parameters for RX Power

This document describes the virtual parameters used in measuring RX power.

## Overview
RX power represents the received signal strength indicator (RSSI) and is crucial for monitoring the quality of the wireless connection.

## Parameters
- **RX Power Level (dBm)**: Represents the strength of the received signal measured in decibels relative to 1 milliwatt.
- **Signal-to-Noise Ratio (SNR)**: A measure that compares the level of a desired signal to the level of background noise.
- **RSSI Threshold**: The minimum acceptable level of RSSI to maintain a reliable connection.

## Usage
These parameters can be used for analytics in network performance and optimization strategies. Adjustments to the system may be influenced by these measurements to ensure connectivity and quality service.
